# AsoSoft Text Corpus

## Summary

The AsoSoft Text Corpus is described as the first large [[Central Kurdish (Sorani)]]
text corpus (188 million tokens), collected and normalized by Hadi Veisi and
colleagues. It contributes the longer-running-text data asset to the Kurdish Data
Explorer, complementing the short headlines of [[Kurdish News Dataset Headlines (KNDH)]].

## Key claims

- ~188 million tokens from websites, books, and magazines.
- Normalized and stored as Text Encoding Initiative (TEI) XML.
- ~22% is topic-annotated with six topic tags.

## Evidence

- Tri-gram language-model perplexity of 276 for Central Kurdish.
- Construction documented in
  [[Toward Kurdish Language Processing: The AsoSoft Text Corpus (Veisi et al. 2019)]],
  including encoding-unification and mixed-script handling.

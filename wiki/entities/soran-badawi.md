# Soran Badawi

## Summary

Soran Badawi is a Kurdish NLP researcher affiliated with the Language Center,
Charmo University (Kurdistan Region, Iraq). He authored two sources central to this
project: the KNDH dataset release and the first transformer NMT model for Sorani.

## Key claims

- Lead/contributing author on the [[Kurdish News Dataset Headlines (KNDH)]] data
  descriptor.
- Author of the first transformer-based neural machine translation model reported
  for Kurdish.

## Evidence

- KNDH paper (Data in Brief 48, 2023) lists S. Badawi among the authors; the
  contact affiliation is Charmo University.
- Sole author of the UHD Journal NMT paper (2023), reporting BLEU 0.45.
